1. Sehat Sahulat Program (SSP) – “Sehat Card / Sehat Card Plus”

Purpose:
Government-funded health insurance providing cashless hospitalization in empanelled public and private hospitals.

Coverage:

Major surgeries, emergency care, maternity, cancer, cardiac, dialysis, transplants, accidents, and chronic illnesses.

Annual coverage limit up to Rs 1,000,000 – Rs 7,000,000 per family (varies by province).

Eligibility Criteria:

Must be a Pakistani citizen with a valid CNIC or B-Form.

Family must be listed in National Socio-Economic Registry (NSER) or Benazir Income Support Program (BISP) database.

In Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (KP) – universal coverage (all residents are eligible).

In Punjab, Sindh, Balochistan, GB, AJK, and Islamabad – coverage is district-wise or for low-income families.

Check Eligibility:
Send your CNIC number (without dashes) to 8500 via SMS.

2. Expanded Programme on Immunization (EPI)

Purpose:
Provides free vaccines to children and mothers to prevent infectious diseases.

Coverage:

Vaccines for polio, tuberculosis (BCG), hepatitis B, DPT, measles, pneumonia, rotavirus, and tetanus.

Available at all Basic Health Units (BHUs), Rural Health Centres (RHCs), and government hospitals.

Eligibility Criteria:

All children aged 0 – 15 months and pregnant women.

No CNIC or income restriction — open to all citizens.

Citizens can check schedules at local EPI centres or via EPI Pakistan mobile app / hotline.

3. Prime Minister’s National Health Program (Merged under SSP)

Purpose:
Initially launched for low-income families for inpatient treatment; now merged into the Sehat Sahulat framework.

Eligibility Criteria:

Families identified as below the poverty line through BISP/NSER.

Holders of valid CNIC residing in covered districts.

4. Punjab Health Initiative Management Company (PHIMC) / Punjab Health Card

Purpose:
Implements the Sehat Sahulat Program for Punjab residents.

Coverage:

Free inpatient care, emergency treatment, maternity services, and surgeries at empanelled hospitals.

Annual limit around Rs 1 million per family.

Eligibility Criteria:

Resident of Punjab province.

Verified through CNIC + NSER/BISP data.

Eligibility check via SMS to 8500.

5. People’s Primary Healthcare Initiative (PPHI) – Sindh & Balochistan

Purpose:
Improves access to primary healthcare in rural and underserved districts.

Coverage:

Free consultations, maternal care, basic lab tests, vaccination, and health awareness.

Eligibility Criteria:

Open to all citizens within designated PPHI areas.

No formal registration required; CNIC or B-Form may be needed for record-keeping.

6. National Maternal, Newborn & Child Health (MNCH) Program

Purpose:
Supports safe deliveries, antenatal checkups, and neonatal care — integrated with Lady Health Worker outreach.

Eligibility Criteria:

Pregnant women and newborns registered with Lady Health Workers or local health facilities.

Free for all citizens.

7. TB Control Program & Hepatitis Control Programs

Purpose:
Provides free diagnosis, medication, and counseling for Tuberculosis and Hepatitis B/C.

Eligibility Criteria:

All citizens diagnosed through government screening centers or partner labs.

CNIC required for record entry; no income restriction.
